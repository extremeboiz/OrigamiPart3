All About Me

Hi, I’m Honey. I am an origami artist/YouTuber. It all began in 2007 when I uploaded my first videos teaching simple origami models, but I only started working seriously on my channel in 2009. In 2010 I got a job and haven't been able to post much really but and I started creating my own models this year. Since then I have created 4 origami on my own, but I still teach also works from other origami artists. Thank You for Supporting me.
